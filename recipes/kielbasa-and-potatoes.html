Kielbasa and Red Potatoes

Description

This is a personal favorite of mine that I came up with myself! This is a simple one pot dish that is easy to create in just a few minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 polish kielbasa
  • 4-5 medium sized red potatoes
  • 1 vidalia onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Slice kielbasa into 1-inch pieces (slice diagonally for more even cooking) and cut red potatoes into 1-inch cubes, leave the skin on or peel depending on personel preference.
  2. Boil a pot of salted water and cook potatoes for ~8 minutes, cooked but still slightly firm. Meanwhile, in a dutch oven, cook kielbasa, onion, and garlic over medium until well browned.
  3. Mix potatoes with kielbasa and cook 3-4 minutes, until potatoes begin to brown.
  4. Wash spinach and cut any stems off, add the bunch of spinach to the pot and cook until spinach is wilted.
  5. Add salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and crushed red pepper and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Let cool 5 minutes and serve.
